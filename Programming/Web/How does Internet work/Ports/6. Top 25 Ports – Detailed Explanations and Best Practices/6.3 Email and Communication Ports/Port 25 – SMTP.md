**Usage:** Mail transfer between servers and initial submission.

**Threats:**
- Open relay abuse
- Injection attacks
- Spam relaying

**Best Practices:**
- Disallow open relays
- Use SMTP authentication
- Implement SPF, DKIM, DMARC
